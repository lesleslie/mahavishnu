## Security Hardening Workflow

Use this workflow to reduce attack surface and close security gaps without disrupting delivery.

## Focus areas

- Threat modeling and asset review
- Prioritized remediation backlog
- Evidence packages and audit support
- Policies, runbooks, and alerts
- Verification of fixes after remediation

## Workflow

1. Review findings, assets, and threat drivers.
1. Rank fixes by risk, exposure, and compliance urgency.
1. Assign owners and remediation windows.
1. Verify fixes with tests, scans, or evidence.
1. Update policies and monitoring after closure.

## Output

- Hardening backlog
- Verified remediation evidence
- Updated operational guidance

- `$ARGUMENTS`
- `$RISK_DRIVERS`
- `$TIMELINE`
