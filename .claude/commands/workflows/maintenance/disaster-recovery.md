## Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Workflow

Use this workflow for catastrophic failures that need executive coordination and recovery execution.

## Focus areas

- Disaster classification and incident command
- Failover or restore strategy
- Customer, legal, and executive communication
- Recovery validation and monitoring
- Post-incident review and prevention

## Workflow

1. Classify the disaster and notify the full response team.
1. Assess business, legal, and technical impact.
1. Pick the appropriate recovery strategy and execute it.
1. Validate critical paths and customer communications.
1. Stabilize, then document lessons learned.

## Output

- Recovery plan and timeline
- Communication and escalation checklist
- Post-disaster follow-up actions

## Inputs

- `$ARGUMENTS`
- `$DISASTER_TYPE`
- `$AFFECTED_REGIONS`
- `$ESTIMATED_DURATION`
