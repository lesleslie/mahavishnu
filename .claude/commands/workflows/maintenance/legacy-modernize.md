## Legacy Modernization Workflow

Use this workflow to modernize legacy systems with staged, low-risk changes.

## Focus areas

- Dependency and architecture discovery
- Incremental delivery and feature flags
- Data migration and parity testing
- Decommission planning

## Workflow

1. Inventory the legacy system and define the target state.
1. Choose the migration strategy and safeguards.
1. Deliver the modernization incrementally with validation.
1. Track parity, data correctness, and rollout readiness.
1. Plan the retirement of old components after cutover.

## Output

- Modernization roadmap
- Data and parity validation checklist
- Decommission plan

## Inputs

- `$ARGUMENTS`
- `$MIGRATION_STRATEGY`
- `$CONSTRAINTS`
