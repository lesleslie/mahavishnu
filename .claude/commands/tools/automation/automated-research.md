## Automated Research System

Use this tool to track external technology trends and produce concise research summaries.

## Focus areas

- Trend monitoring and competitive analysis
- Research report generation
- Archive and storage handling

## Workflow

1. Choose the sources and topics to monitor.
1. Collect trend data on a schedule.
1. Summarize emerging tools, practices, and gaps.
1. Archive older reports and refresh the categories periodically.

## Output

- Trend or research report
- Recommendations and caveats
- Maintenance checklist
