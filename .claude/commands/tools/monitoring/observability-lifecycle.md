## Observability Lifecycle Guide

Use this tool to plan telemetry, alerts, debugging, and analytics as one lifecycle.

## Focus areas

- Instrumentation coverage and retention
- Alerting and on-call routing
- Debugging and incident response hooks
- Performance and usage analytics

## Workflow

1. Inventory metrics, logs, and traces.
1. Define alerting, routing, and escalation rules.
1. Add debugging and evidence-capture guidance.
1. Use analytics to drive optimization and cost decisions.

## Output

- Observability readiness checklist
- Alert and escalation matrix
- Dashboard and optimization notes
