## Accessibility Audit and Testing

Use this tool to audit accessibility, identify barriers, and prioritize remediation.

## Focus areas

- WCAG coverage and assistive-tech compatibility
- Automated and manual testing
- Component-level issues and page-level patterns
- Remediation guidance and verification
- Ongoing accessibility practice

## Workflow

1. Run automated audits against the target page or component set.
1. Manually verify critical flows with accessibility tools.
1. Group findings by severity and user impact.
1. Provide remediation guidance and retest after fixes.
1. Capture follow-up checks for future releases.

## Output

- Accessibility findings and score summary
- Prioritized remediation checklist
- Retest and monitoring notes
