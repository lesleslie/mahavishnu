## Regulatory Compliance Check

Use this tool to assess compliance posture and identify practical control gaps.

## Focus areas

- Regulation mapping: GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2, PCI-DSS
- Privacy controls and audit trails
- Access control and data retention
- Security evidence and logging
- Implementation guidance for remediation

## Workflow

1. Determine which regulations apply.
1. Check whether controls exist and are testable.
1. Look for missing logs, retention gaps, or unsafe data handling.
1. Prioritize fixes by risk and audit impact.
1. Keep the output actionable for engineering and operations.

## Output

- Applicable regulations and rationale
- Compliance gaps and evidence requests
- Prioritized remediation checklist
