## Release Management Playbook

Use this tool to plan, approve, launch, and stabilize releases.

## Focus areas

- Release calendar and freeze windows
- Go/no-go criteria and sign-offs
- Rollback and contingency planning
- Communication templates and stakeholder updates
- Post-release metrics and follow-up actions

## Workflow

1. Confirm scope, risks, and release window.
1. Collect QA, security, support, and product evidence.
1. Decide go/no-go and prepare rollback steps.
1. Execute deployment with live monitoring.
1. Capture outcomes and retro actions after launch.

## Output

- Release readiness checklist
- Stakeholder communication brief
- Rollback and stabilization plan

## Inputs

- `$RELEASE_NAME`
- `$SCOPE`
- `$RISKS`
