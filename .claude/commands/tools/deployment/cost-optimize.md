## Cloud Cost Optimization

Use this tool to find spend reduction opportunities without degrading reliability.

## Focus areas

- Service-level cost visibility
- Waste detection and idle resources
- Rightsizing and instance selection
- Storage and snapshot cleanup
- Commitment and reservation strategy
- Guardrails for future spend

## Workflow

1. Identify the cloud provider and time window.
1. Break down cost by service and resource.
1. Call out waste, idle assets, and oversized resources.
1. Prioritize quick wins before deeper architecture changes.
1. Include rollback or safety notes for any automated savings action.

## Output

- Cost summary and top drivers
- Savings opportunities with rough impact
- Recommended next actions by priority
