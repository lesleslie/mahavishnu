## Support Readiness Runbook

Use this tool to prepare support teams for a launch, incident, or customer-impacting change.

## Focus areas

- Support signals and trends
- Enablement and communication plans
- Escalation paths and SLAs
- Metrics, dashboards, and follow-up

## Workflow

1. Review support, chat, and telemetry signals.
1. Prepare enablement assets and comms.
1. Define escalation workflow and response targets.
1. Track adoption and feed learnings back into delivery.

## Output

- Support readiness checklist
- Escalation matrix
- Enablement and dashboard notes

## Inputs

- `$CUSTOMER_SEGMENT`
- `$RELEASE_OR_INCIDENT`
- `$METRICS`
