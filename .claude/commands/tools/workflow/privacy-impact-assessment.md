## Privacy Impact Assessment Toolkit

Use this tool to evaluate privacy risk, data flows, and regulatory obligations before launch.

## Focus areas

- Data classification and jurisdiction mapping
- Risk evaluation and mitigations
- Control planning and approvals
- Audit-ready documentation

## Workflow

1. Scope the project and identify data categories.
1. Assess privacy risks and required controls.
1. Document mitigations and approval records.
1. Store the final assessment for audit readiness.

## Output

- Privacy impact assessment report
- Mitigation backlog
- Approval log

## Inputs

- `$INITIATIVE`
- `$DATA_CATEGORIES`
- `$JURISDICTIONS`
