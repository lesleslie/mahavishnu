## Message Queue Integration

Use this tool to design async messaging with the right queue, routing, and reliability model.

## Focus areas

- Technology selection
- Messaging patterns and schemas
- Reliability and retry behavior
- Performance, scaling, and backpressure
- Monitoring and consumer health

## Workflow

1. Choose the queue technology based on throughput and routing needs.
1. Define the message contract and delivery guarantees.
1. Add retries, dead-letter handling, and idempotency.
1. Plan partitioning, batching, and scaling behavior.
1. Monitor lag, errors, and consumer health.

## Output

- Queue selection recommendation
- Message design and reliability checklist
- Monitoring and scaling notes
