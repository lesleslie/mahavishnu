## Content Management Workflows

Use this tool to plan content systems, localization, validation, and publishing automation.

## Focus areas

- Content models, taxonomy, and versioning
- Editorial workflows and approvals
- Localization and multi-language support
- SEO, validation, and compliance checks
- Publishing and distribution automation
- Analytics and feedback loops

## Workflow

1. Identify the content types and workflow stages.
1. Choose the storage and publishing model.
1. Define review, localization, and approval steps.
1. Add validation, SEO, and analytics only where they help.
1. Keep the implementation aligned with the repo’s CMS or content stack.

## Output

- Content workflow design
- Suggested architecture and automation steps
- Validation and publishing checklist
