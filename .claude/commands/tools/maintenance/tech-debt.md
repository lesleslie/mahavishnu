## Technical Debt Analysis and Remediation

Use this tool to identify, quantify, and prioritize technical debt with a practical remediation plan.

## Focus areas

- Code, architecture, testing, and documentation debt
- Deployment and infrastructure debt
- Impact and ROI framing
- Metrics and prioritization

## Workflow

1. Inventory the main forms of debt in the repo or system.
1. Estimate the cost of leaving each debt item unresolved.
1. Rank the work by risk, impact, and effort.
1. Turn the highest-value items into a remediation plan.

## Output

- Debt inventory and scoring
- Prioritized remediation plan
- KPI or dashboard notes
