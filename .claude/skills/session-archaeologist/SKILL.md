# Session Archaeologist

## Overview

Use this skill to recover decisions, solutions, and context from past sessions across the Bodai ecosystem.

## When to Use

- Recovering a past decision and its rationale
- Finding how a problem was solved before
- Reconstructing session history
- Tracing the evolution of a change over time

## Core Rule

- Recover context, not just search results.

## Quick Reference

- Search: `mcp__akosha__search_all_systems`
- Enrich: `mcp__akosha__query_knowledge_graph`

## Notes

- Reformulate conversational questions into search-friendly terms.
- Synthesize results into a short narrative with decision, rationale, and outcome.
